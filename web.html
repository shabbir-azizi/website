Pakistan's Real Hero & nulcear

Dr. Abdul Qadeer Khan (A.Q. Khan) was a Pakistani scientist and metallurgical engineer known for his pivotal role in Pakistan's nuclear program. He is often credited as the "father of Pakistan's nuclear bomb" and is considered a hero in Pakistan. However, he is also a controversial figure, with some viewing him as a nuclear proliferator for his role in helping other nations develop their own nuclear weapons programs.
